# E-Commerce Sales Analytics & RFM Customer Segmentation Dashboard

**Python | SQLite | Power BI**

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **779K+ e-commerce transactions** to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, revenue drivers, and customer retention opportunities.

Using **Python, SQLite, and Power BI**, the project transforms raw transaction data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, customer segmentation, and interactive dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

The company lacked visibility into:

- High-value customers
- Customer retention opportunities
- Revenue-driving products
- Geographic sales performance
- Customer purchasing behavior

The objective was to build a business intelligence solution to support data-driven decision making.

## Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among a small percentage of customers.
- United Kingdom contributes the largest share of revenue.
- A small set of products drives a significant proportion of sales.
- Customer segmentation helps identify retention opportunities.
- High-value customers contribute disproportionately to business growth.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus retention efforts on Champion customers.
- Re-engage At-Risk customers through targeted campaigns.
- Prioritize inventory planning for top-performing products.
- Optimize marketing spend toward high-value customer segments.
